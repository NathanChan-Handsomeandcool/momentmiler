About

A Collector of Moments

Hi — I’m someone who loves to travel and embrace whatever experiences the world leads me to.

Most of my time is spent planning small trips, learning about different places, and quietly collecting moments along the way.

What you’ll find here are real moments. Some ordinary, some priceless — but all meaningful to me.

Not every photograph is perfect, but every image holds a feeling that brings me back to a memory I never want to lose.

The places I’ve been, the people I’ve met, and the silence in between have taught me more about myself than I ever expected.

I chose photography because it’s the only way I know how to hold onto these fleeting moments — to remember what life felt like while I was living it.

— Netiwat C.
