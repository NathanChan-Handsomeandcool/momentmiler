About

A traveler with a camera.

Hi — I'm a landscape photographer based somewhere between sea level and altitude. I spend most of the year planning small trips, learning to read weather, and waiting for light that often doesn't come.

What you'll find here are the ones that did. Iceland's quiet south coast, the soft greens of Hokkaido, the granite cathedrals of Patagonia — places that asked me to be patient, and rewarded me anyway.

I shoot mostly with a mirrorless body and a small bag of primes. Most images here are made within a few minutes of either sunrise or sunset, often alone, almost always on foot.
